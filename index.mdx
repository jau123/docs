title: "MeiGen"
description: "AI-powered image generation platform with multi-model support"

MeiGen is an AI image generation platform that brings together multiple state-of-the-art models in one place. Browse a curated gallery of AI artwork, generate your own images, and integrate with your favorite AI coding tools via our MCP plugin.

## What you can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Browse the Gallery" icon="images" href="/features/gallery">
    Explore thousands of curated AI-generated images. Save favorites, search by keyword, and use any image as inspiration for your own creations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Generate Images" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/features/generate">
    Create images with multiple AI models. Upload reference images, adjust aspect ratios, and use prompt enhancement to get better results.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compare Models" icon="scale-balanced" href="/features/models">
    Choose from 8 image and video models — each with different strengths, costs, and supported features.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use the API" icon="code" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    Integrate MeiGen into your workflow. Generate images programmatically with our REST API or MCP plugin.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
